What exactly is social media marketing and how can it help my Pune business?

Social media marketing involves creating and sharing content on platforms like Facebook, Instagram, and LinkedIn to promote your business. For Pune businesses, it's a cost-effective way to reach local customers, build brand awareness, and drive sales. Unlike traditional advertising, social media lets you target specific neighborhoods, age groups, and interests.

How much should I budget for social media marketing in Pune?

Our social media marketing packages Pune start from ₹15,000/month for basic services. Most small to medium businesses invest ₹25,000-40,000/month for comprehensive services that include content creation, advertising, and management. The investment typically pays for itself through increased sales within 3-6 months.

Which social media platforms work best for businesses in Pune?

It depends on your target audience.

  • Facebook: Great for all businesses, especially restaurants and local services
  • Instagram: Perfect for fashion, food, lifestyle, and visual businesses
  • LinkedIn: Essential for B2B companies, especially in Hinjewadi and Baner IT areas
  • YouTube: Excellent for education, healthcare, and service-based businesses

How long before I see results from social media marketing?

Most businesses see initial improvements in engagement and followers within 30 days. Significant lead generation and sales growth typically occur within 3-6 months. However, some businesses see immediate results from targeted advertising campaigns within the first few weeks.
